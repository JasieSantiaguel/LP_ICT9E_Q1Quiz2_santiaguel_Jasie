SPIRITED AWAY

by Ghibli Studios

Spirited away is a Japanese animated movie about a young girl named Chihiro,
who accidentally enters a world with spirits of the Japanese Shinto Folklore (Kami)
while exploring a mysterious tunnel in her neighborhood with her parents.

A gist of the movie:

Chihiro and her parents explored the spirit world and
her parents found a table filled with delicious foods.
Chihiro sensed something off about the stack of food
infront of them, let alone the weird area they were in. She told her parents not to
eat the food but they gobbled it all up at instant. A few seconds
after, Chihiro started to notice that
they were slowly turning into pigs.She got scared since her parents turned into pigs, and it was getting dark,
so she ran. Thats when Haku, the second main character, comes in.

The voice actors

  • Miyu Irino
  • Rumi Hiiragi
